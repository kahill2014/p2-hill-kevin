Frequently Asked Questions

About the shelter

What are your hours/where are you located?
182 W. Main Street, Whitewater, Wisconsin, 53190. Our hours are Noon-4PM on Saturdays or by appointment during the week.
Do you take surrenders?
No. Touched By A Paw is strictly focused towards the care and sheltering of stray, neglected, and/or abused cats. We do not take in surrendered cats.
How much are your adoption fees?
Click here to see our adoption fees.
How old do you have to be to adopt?
Our minimum age limit is twenty four, but we do make exceptions. Additional information can be found here.
Will you watch/board my cat while I'm on vacation?
We do not offer a boarding option at our shelter. This is because we do not have the space, and we have had issues in the past with owners not returning to pick up their cat and abandoning them.

About the cats

Why is my cat peeing outside of the litter box/around the house?
The answer to this, and many issues regarding behavioral problems can be found here, or on the website Cats International. Issues including litter box problems, scratching, aggression, and proper cat care can be found there.
How long do you keep the cats?
We keep our rescues until they find their forever home.
How can I spay/neuter inexpensively?
Touched by a Paw recommends the Spay Me! Clinic in Madison, Wisconsin. Click the name to visit their website.
